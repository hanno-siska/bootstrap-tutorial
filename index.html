Inside the book...

A closer look at frogs, habitats, and their hidden lives

Frogs begin their journey in quiet ponds, where tadpoles grow, adapt, and learn to survive in shifting waters.

From rainforests to marshes, frogs adapt to some of the most diverse ecosystems on Earth.

The night comes alive with croaks, calls, and mating songs echoing across wetlands.

Camouflage, toxins, and jumping power make frogs surprisingly advanced survivors.

From glowing frogs to hidden species in remote jungles, nature still holds many secrets.
